Plantation Day

On Friday, 21th March’ 2019 Woodland Pre School celebrated Plantation Day to create awareness among the young minds about the invaluable resource on our planet – Plants and Trees. The little ones were taught about the importance of the trees for our environment and the ecosystem. Children were explained in detail about the process of sowing the seeds, the various phases undergone in becoming plants and also about the importance of air, sunlight, water and soil in the plant growth. Later, they were given seeds to sow by themselves in soil and watered them under the guidance of their worthy teachers. They were thrilled to get involved in this interesting activity. It was a great pleasure seeing our tiny Pre Schoolers being gardeners for a day.
